I0 Checklist - Process

Process

Requirements
  1. Has the team reviewed and agreed to the Requirements Management Approach (RMA) work product?
  2. Have you completed enough story card backlog to start iteration 1?
  3. Will you be able to maintain a healthy backlog that will sustain work for the team?
Testing
  1. Is there a test strategy in place between the line and the project team? Test strategy should include all applicable methods: Unit, acceptance, integration, system, regression and performance testing.
    1. If no, do you plan on completing a test strategy? Please explain.
  2. Has the line identified and addressed test data needs?
Agile
  1. Have you established a line cadence?
    1. If you are changing it please explain.
Team
  1. Has the overall timeline and estimate been compared to the project funding estimate?
  2. Have you baselined the release plan with the project timeline and team velocity?
  3. Have the ADL and PM reviewed and agreed to the release plan?
  4. Will the team provide a release commitment and a revised estimate at the commit gate (40% of DDIT)?
  5. Has the warranty support period been considered in the team's estimate?
  6. Do all team members have tasks in Clarity to bill time?
  7. Is the ADC spot site updated to reflect current project demand?
  8. Is there a need to deviate from standard work? If so, has it been requested through a waiver?
  9. Will the Nationwide Line be conducting build to run transition?
    1. If no, is there an agreement in place with the BSA App Suite Owner? Please explain.
Risk
  1. Has the project team and the line collaborated on the risks and agreed on the appropriate risk-based contingency (financial and schedule) needed for the identified scope?
  2. Are there any risks that prevent the team from starting work on the project?
    1. If yes, is there a plan to mitigate the risk?
